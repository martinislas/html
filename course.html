Course Title: Owning Property As A Foreigner in Cambodia - An Introduction to Asia's Emerging Real Estate Economy

Course Summary:

This course provides an in-depth understanding of property ownership for foreigners in Cambodia, including the different types of property titles, their legal implications, and the rights and limitations associated with each title. Additionally, it explores why Cambodia is an attractive destination for real estate investment, highlighting its emerging economy and the opportunities it presents.

Course Syllabus:

Topic 1: Introduction to Cambodia as an Emerging Business & Logistics Hub

  • Lesson 1: Why Cambodia? Exploring the Economic Outlook and GDP Growth
  • Lesson 2: Core Industries & SEZs in Cambodia: Opportunities for Foreign Investors
  • Lesson 3: Political Support & Stability: Factors Influencing Property Ownership

Topic 2: Understanding the Cambodian Healthcare System and Technology Investments

  • Lesson 1: Overview of Cambodia's Healthcare System and Services
  • Lesson 2: Technology & Other Investments: Impact on Property Ownership
  • Lesson 3: Exploring the Role of Technology in Property Management

Topic 3: Exploring Tourism and its Impact on Property Ownership

  • Lesson 1: Understanding the Importance of Tourism in Cambodia's Economy
  • Lesson 2: Airports, Infrastructure & Connectivity: Enhancing Property Investment
  • Lesson 3: Analyzing Property and Land Prices in Phnom Penh

Topic 4: Property Titles Explained

  • Lesson 1: Introduction to Property Ownership Laws in Cambodia
  • Lesson 2: Understanding Hard Titles and their Importance
  • Lesson 3: Exploring Soft Titles and their Limitations

Topic 5: Private Ownership in Co-owned Buildings (Strata Title)

  • Lesson 1: Overview of Strata Title Ownership for Foreigners
  • Lesson 2: Understanding the Percentage Ownership Restrictions
  • Lesson 3: Legal Considerations and Rights of Foreign Owners

Topic 6: LMAP Titles and their Benefits

  • Lesson 1: Introduction to LMAP Titles and their Significance
  • Lesson 2: Exploring the Role of GPS Coordinates in LMAP Titles
  • Lesson 3: Comparing LMAP Titles with other Property Titles

Topic 7: Factors to Consider When Choosing a Property Title

  • Lesson 1: Evaluating the Security and Reliability of Different Property Titles
  • Lesson 2: Legal Implications and Risks Associated with Each Title
  • Lesson 3: Making Informed Decisions based on Individual Needs and Circumstances

Topic 8: Recent Developments in Cambodia's Property Ownership Laws

  • Lesson 1: Overview of Recent Changes in Property Ownership Regulations
  • Lesson 2: Impact of Foreign Tourist Arrivals on Property Market
  • Lesson 3: Understanding the Economic Factors Influencing Property Ownership

Topic 9: Practical Tips for Foreign Property Owners in Cambodia

  • Lesson 1: Navigating Property Taxes and Fees as a Foreign Owner
  • Lesson 2: Engaging Reliable Property Management Services
  • Lesson 3: Exploring Opportunities and Challenges in the Cambodian Real Estate Market

Note: The syllabus is designed to be completed in 10 hours, with each lesson taking approximately 30 minutes. The additional context provided highlights the economic factors, healthcare system, technology investments, tourism, and infrastructure in Cambodia, which are relevant to property ownership considerations. The course also explores why Cambodia is an attractive destination for real estate investment, providing a comprehensive understanding of the country's emerging real estate economy.
